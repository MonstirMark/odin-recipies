Crazy Pizza

A flat, open-faced baked pie of Italian origin,

consisting of a thin layer of bread dough topped

with spiced bechamel sauce and cheese, garnished

with spinach, assorted cheeses and mushrooms.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil or to taste
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, rinsed and dried
  • 8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
